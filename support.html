Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How will the points system work in the app ?

Ans: Each user will be given 200 points at the start of every month in their account. You can use it to bid for the courts. Also points of previous month will be carried over to subsequent month's balance.

Q: How does the bidding system work?

Ans: For each time slot students will place their bids, then after mentioned time, the bidding period will be over and then the student with second highest bid will be alotted the court for that particular time slot.
